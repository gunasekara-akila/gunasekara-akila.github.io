SHEET 01 / ABOUTProfile

I'm a Civil & Structural Engineer with over two years of professional experience in structural design, site supervision, quality control and project coordination of RCC and steel structures. I've worked across both design-office and site-based engineering roles, using ETABS, SAP2000, Prokon and Tekla Structural Designer for analysis and design, alongside Bentley WaterGEMS and SewerGEMS for water and wastewater network modelling.

My day-to-day work includes reviewing drawings, monitoring construction quality, preparing technical reports and calculations, and coordinating with contractors and consultants to keep projects compliant with specifications and engineering standards. I've also represented my firm on an international project — the Rajshahi WASA Water Supply Project in Bangladesh — attending client meetings and site visits as part of the design review team.

Outside the office, I run a small freelance structural design practice for two-storey residential houses, and I hold Associate Membership of both the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL) and the Engineering Council Sri Lanka (ECSL). I'm currently open to structural / design engineering roles with international firms, including relocation or remote collaboration.
